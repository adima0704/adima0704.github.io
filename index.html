About Team Inspiration . . .

Team Inspiration is a garage-based robotics team in San Diego, California. They participated in FIRST Tech Challenge and have competed in the world championship twice. They are now looking forward to competing in RoboSub. Along with robotics, Team Inspiration has completed thousands of hours of outreach to local and global communities. Learn more about the team.

RoboSub

After 8 years of anticipation and planning, Team Inspiration has finally gotten involved in RoboSub, an international underwater robotics program in which teams complete missions using autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs).
Learn more here >

FTC

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics program for students in grades 7-12. Team Inspiration (#11128) has been involved in FTC for 3 years, and competed in the world championship in 2017 and 2018.
